WHAT IS
COVID-19

Coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) is a respiratory illness that can spread from person to person. The virus that causes COVID-19 is a novel coronavirus that was first identified during an investigation into an outbreak in Wuhan, China. The primary mode of infection in humans is human-to-human transmission, which generally occurs via respiratory droplets from infected individuals which they sneeze, cough or expire. Transmission can be limited by washing hands, good hygiene, and wearing surgical masks. learn more

How to Prevent
Covid19

  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds especially after you have been in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing.
  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick Put distance between yourself and other people if COVID-19 is spreading in your community.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily like tables, doorknobs, light switches, handles, phone etc.
  • You should wear a facemask if you are sick.If you are not sick, you should not need to wear a facemask unless you are caring for someone who is sick
  • Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze or use the inside of your elbow.
  • Keep your hands and fingers away from your eyes, nose, and mouth.
